Hi there! My name is Niko Drossos, and I'm a front-end developer with a passion for creating beautiful, functional websites. My journey as a web developer began with learning HTML and CSS, and I quickly fell in love with the power of these languages to transform ideas into fully-realized web designs.

In my portfolio, you'll find a selection of my favorite projects from my time as a front-end developer. Each project showcases my approach to web design and development, from the early stages of wireframing and prototyping to the final touches of polished CSS animations and responsive layouts.
